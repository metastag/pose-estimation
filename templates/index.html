How It Works

The PT Progress Tracker uses MediaPipe's pose estimation to extract 33 3D body landmarks from your exercise video. Joint angles are computed and compared against clinically-informed gold standard templates with weighted scoring that accounts for the clinical significance of each joint. Errors in critical joints like the knee or spine are penalized more heavily than peripheral joints. The system identifies the top deviations and maps them to human-readable feedback cues, helping you correct your form in real time.
