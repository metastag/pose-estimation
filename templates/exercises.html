Supported Exercises

Gold Standard Approach

Each exercise is defined by a gold standard skeletal template sourced from sessions performed by certified physical therapists under controlled conditions. Joint angles, tolerances, and clinically-weighted scoring ensure that feedback is grounded in professional expertise rather than purely data-driven heuristics. Key joints involved in each exercise receive higher weight in the scoring function, reflecting their clinical significance for that specific movement pattern.
